<h3>Chinese New Year Dinner 2025</h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/CNY_2025_Poster__1_.png">
</div>
<p>Join us for an unforgettable Changi Simei CNY Dinner celebration!</p>
<p></p>
<p>Date: 11 January 2025 (Saturday)</p>
<p>Time: 7 PM</p>
<p>Venue: Bliss Garden Restaurant, Singapore Expo Foyer 1, 1 Expo Drive #02-03,
S(486150)</p>
<p>Indulge in a sumptuous 8-course dinner for just $48 per person or $480
for a table of ten.</p>
<p>Enjoy a variety of dishes with Halal and Vegetarian options available
for whole table bookings only.</p>
<p>Ticket Sales Start: 2 December 2024</p>
<p>Payment Method: Cash only at Changi Simei Community Centre.</p>
<p>Don't miss out on this festive gathering! Book early to secure your spot
and celebrate the Lunar New Year with family and friends in a vibrant atmosphere
filled with joy and laughter.</p>

<h4><strong>Our History</strong></h4>
<p><strong>1960 – 1980&nbsp;– Hainan Village</strong>
</p>
<p>Prior to the houses one see in Sunbird Apollo Estate, this area was a
tropical fish farming village. Due to the large number of Hainanese living
in the village, it was often referred as “Hainan Village”. In fact, several
original villagers still live in the estate till today.</p>
<p>In the 60s and 70s, this area was also referred as Changi 10 Miles. Before
Simei HDB estate was built, much activities was centred near the old Changi
Simei CC located opposite the present Shell Station along Upper Changi
Road East.</p>
